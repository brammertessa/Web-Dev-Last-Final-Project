About Me

My name is Tessa Brammer and I am from the East Coast. I have been a Wedding Coordinator for three years now so, yes, I have met a handful of Bride-zillas. They're only mean if you don't do what they ask, so to keep the peace, just do it! My favorite part however is working as a florist! I haven't had any experience before this job, and I love it more and more everyday.

  1. Best tips to keep your flowers fresh!
    • Store in a cool place, a floral fridge is the best
    • When you recieve flowers, cut the stems and get fresh water, bonus if you have floral food
  2. My favorite floral trends
    • Specialty flowers in bouquets
    • Assymetrical arrangments
